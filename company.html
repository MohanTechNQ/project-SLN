About Us

We feel great to introduce ourselves as SLN uPVC Doors & Windows, the European standard solutions. Our aim is to provide completely dust-free, durable, noise insulation, waterproof, energy efficient doors and windows to our customers, thus enhancing their experience & trust in our brand. We are the 1st company to introduce & manufacture European standard uPVC Doors and Windows in Coastal Karnataka of India.

We are based at Koteshwar village of Udupi District in Karnataka. We initiated this operation in 2016 with a capacity of forty windows per day. Our SLN team works in an area spread across 6,000 sq. ft. factory premise. We have state of art infrastructure with all the modern technology to produce quality material. The growth in our DGK Group business is a clear indication of the acceptance of our quality products as a trusted brand. Our group has a history of 32 years of various business and manufacturing arrangements.

The complete uPVC systems and profiles are brought to you in technical collaboration with Deceuninck of Belgium in Europe, which is supposed to be the World’s No.2 ranked among uPVC profile manufacturers. In order to ensure consistency in the quality of our products, our engineers keep constantly updated themselves with innovations & technologies practiced. These profiles are highly durable in nature. This makes our products highly strong, durable, value loaded and acceptable product in Coastal Karnataka.

A combination of European Profiles, accurate cutting, welding & cornering machinery of Italian make as well as the use of latest technology & practices helps in delivering doors & windows of high quality, energy efficient as well as environmentally safe to use. Our production team is highly efficient & they check all the essential parameters before sending out any product. Our aim is to fulfill the demand for quality doors and windows in India at affordable prices.
